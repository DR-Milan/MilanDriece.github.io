About me:

My name is Milan Driece. I am an enthusiastic, hardworking guy, and I always try my very best. I work at Domino's and CLS. I study game development at Grafisch Lyceum Utrecht. In my free time, I mostly play video games like The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom and a lot of first-person shooters. I also enjoy hanging out with family and friends.

I have worked with Unity C# for 3 years. I also have experience with Unreal Engine and Unreal Blueprints for about 6 months. I know how to use GitHub to save and collaborate on projects. Additionally, I'm good at editing videos, and I've made all the videos on my YouTube channel myself.

I understand the scrum method for managing projects well. I'm good at leading a team and enjoy working with others. I'm also good at recognizing the strengths of my teammates and making sure everyone is happy and having a good time on a project.

I'm quick to learn the basics of new things. For example, I learned Unity multiplayer in just a few weeks, and now I understand it well enough to teach others. I really enjoy helping others too.
